'Ice Cream' is the debut solo single from ITZY's Yuna. This record from the group's *maknae* (youngest) is the second solo release from ITZY members, following on from Yeji's EP last year. Grab your pastel-coloured outfits, this song is quality bubblegum pop from start to finish. The arrangement is dripping with 1980s vibes, including the classic snare drum, synth brass stabs, and guitar with heavy chorus and reverb.

The verse structure is simple and effective, and the pre-chorus builds nicely into the sparkly chorus. Aside from the questionable use of 'sweety' as an adjective, the lyrics are cutesy and effective, describing the transience of time and love in terms of ice cream - as they can quickly melt and be gone before you know it.
