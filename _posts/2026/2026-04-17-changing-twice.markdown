One of the great things about being a '*Once*' (a Twice fan), is that there is a seemingly endless catalogue of music to explore. This week I found an absolute bop that I'd not heard before. 'Changing!' is a b-side from their second Japanese studio album '&Twice'. While this album may have lost the 'K' it certinaly maintains the 'pop' - it's full of catchy songs with many pop arrangements to back them up.

'Changing!' leans into dance-pop and disco-pop with a four on the floor beat and a rich arrangement, particularly catch string runs and stabs brining the disco influence, and the piano really anchoring the pop feel.
