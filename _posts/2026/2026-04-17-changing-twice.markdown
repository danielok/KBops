One of the great things about being a '*Once*' (a Twice fan), is that there is a seemingly endless catalogue of music to explore. This week I found an absolute bop that I'd not heard before. 'Changing!' is a B-side from their second Japanese studio album '&Twice'. While this album may have not have the 'K' it certainly maintains the 'pop' - it's full of catchy songs with tasty arrangements to back them up.

'Changing!' starts with an intriguing filtered-guitar intro, then immediately leans into dance-pop with a four on the floor beat. The catchy string and brass stabs bring the disco influence, and the deep piano anchors the pop feel of this rich arrangement. The lyrics bring a bit of J-pop influence: the obligatory '*tokimeki*' is there!

Unfortunately, there's no music video, or even official performance clip of this one, but just set your speaker to maximum and dance along - you'll have to make up your own choreography!
