Time for the catchiest song of the year so far! 'Catch Catch' by Yena will be stuck in your head as soon as you hear it. The Korean spelling for title for this song is '캐치 캐치', which is pronounced 'kechi kechi', which is even more fitting.

This song's vibe takes a step back into the older, cuter, bubble-gum K-pop days of the past, but with a modern-sounding arrangement. The result is a groovy, high energy dance track, that builds throughout the song. 

The music video is equally colourful and delightful, and deeply unserious, but still keeps a modern aesthetic. It doesn't bring back the over-exposure/over-saturation, plain white 'void' backgrounds, or crop-zoomed headshots. It does have a cute Dance Dance Revolution shot during the phone key tone bridge.

Check it out, and you'll be singing along 'daaaah da da da da' before you know it.
