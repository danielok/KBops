Grab the mirror ball and the smoke machine, Lee Chaeyeon is here with a K-Disco Pop track. Known for her time in IZ*One and also as ITZY Lee Chaereyong's older sister, she has had mixed success in her solo career.

While on the surface it seems to be a straightforward disco-pop song, beneath the soaring strings and the thumping bass there is a more serious undertone carrying the message of Chaeyeon's difficult few years and ultimate return to the stage where she belongs.
