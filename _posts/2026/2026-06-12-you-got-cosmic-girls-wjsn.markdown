Let's wind the clock back to 2019, and listen to this electro-pop masterpiece: 'You Got' from Cosmic Girls (also known as WJSN which is from the English initals of the Korean words for Cosmic Girls 우주소녀 *woo-joo so-nyeo*).

The intro starts with a classic filter sweep over the drums, synth and bass. As the verse kicks in the vocals balance out the snarling synth bass nicely. I feel like this arragement would be something like what we'd expect from Chromeo if they ever produced for a K-Pop group.

Lots of syncopation of the synths and vocals carry the song nicely, and some accented guitar riffs add to this an upbeat track. Overall it's a little bit different from your standard K-Pop dance pop track, a nice twist.
