Let's reassemble the dancefloor from last week, as we'll need it again for this week's slightly less intense, but no less catchy dance track: '5!6!7!8!'. Nicole's maths class is in session with this bop that is smoother than a sine wave.

More than just a 'by the numbers' dance track, 5!6!7!8! deviates from the standard with above-average vocals and a well-rounded arrangement. The intro is a classic dance music filter sweep, with plenty of synths, a solid bass, and four-on-the-floor beat to get the momentum going, no need for mass or acceleration here. 

The crowd vocal effect on the '5!6!7!8!' line in the chorus made me think I was listening to the live version by mistake, but it fits well in the studio version, adding to the already catchy chorus.

My favourite part is the post-chorus, which raises the maxima of this track to 10/10 (literally).

The M/V is very stylish, and the dance challenge videos are cheeky and fun!
