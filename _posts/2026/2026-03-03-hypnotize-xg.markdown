This week we have an absolute dance-floor destroyer: 'Hypnotize' from XG. This song is melodic vocal house with a dash of trance. Wouldn't go astray on a 2000s Ministry of Sound compilation.

The production is classic house and trance: rhythmic house pianos, synths, and 909 drums samples, with breakbeats in the breakdowns. The vocals take more of modern k-pop feel, with melodic verses and a smooth chorus, broken up by syncopated rap style sections. And of course we have some classic trance whispers in the bridge.

There is some debate if XG are a in fact a K-pop group: they have all Japanese members, and sing in English, but were trained in Seoul, produce with Korean staff, and promote on Korean music shows. In any case, they are certainly K-pop adjacent enough for a feature on K-Bops!
